Rewards for healthy lifestyle choices

Geo-Incentives enables disadvantaged high school students in the Bay Area to earn points by "checking in" to healthy, constructive activities. Points can be redeemed for laptops, plane tickets, or some good ol' cash via Student PayPal!

Who Is Eligible

Our rewards are reserved for students without readily available financial supports or opportunities to grow. We focus on low-income students aged 13 to 18. If you think you meet this criteria, please apply using the below link and we'll get back to you within 24 hours!

Sponsor A Reward

Our rewards marketplace provides businesses with an opportunity to meet their financial and social bottom lines. By sponsoring a reward, you can change a life and raise brand awareness among young people. Nonprofits can also use our reward marketplace to distribute goods.

Help Nonprofits Fundraise

Youth are not the only ones being rewarded by our product. Nonprofits spend huge amounts of time and money tracking participant behavior. For the first time ever, nonprofits can use data submitted and collected by youth themselves to satisfy funding requirements.
